# Raani Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Raani is committed to protecting the privacy of users who interact with our application and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in line with the Nigeria Data Protection Act, 2023 and general privacy practices applicable to Nigerian digital services.

## Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that users provide directly or through use of the service, including:

- Name and contact details, such as phone number, email address, and delivery address.
- Order and transaction details, such as selected products, purchase history, payment status, and delivery information.
- WhatsApp or messaging details required to respond to requests and provide customer support.
- Device, log, and technical information, such as IP address, browser type, access time, and usage activity.
- Any other information voluntarily submitted when contacting us or using our services.

## How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Create, manage, and support user interactions with Raani.
- Process orders, deliveries, payments, reminders, and customer requests.
- Communicate with users about orders, updates, support, promotions, or service changes.
- Improve the reliability, security, and performance of the application.
- Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and other harmful activity.
- Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, and lawful business obligations.

## Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal information based on one or more lawful grounds, including user consent, performance of a contract or requested service, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interest in operating, improving, and protecting the service.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell users' personal information. We may share information only where necessary with:

- Service providers who help us operate the application, process payments, deliver orders, send messages, host data, or provide customer support.
- Business partners involved in fulfilling user requests or orders.
- Law enforcement, regulators, courts, or public authorities where required by law.
- Professional advisers where necessary for business, legal, audit, or compliance purposes.

Any third party that processes personal information on our behalf is expected to protect it and use it only for the purpose for which it was shared.

## Data Storage and Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, comply with legal obligations, and maintain business records. When information is no longer required, we will delete, anonymize, or securely store it in line with applicable requirements.

## Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security, and users should also protect their devices, passwords, and account access.

## User Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may have the right to:

- Request access to their personal information.
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
- Request deletion of personal information where legally permitted.
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
- Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
- Request transfer of personal information where applicable.
- Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission or another appropriate authority.

To exercise these rights, users may contact us using the contact details below.

## Cookies and Tracking

Where our website or application uses cookies, analytics, or similar technologies, they may be used to remember preferences, understand usage, improve performance, and protect the service. Users may be able to disable cookies through their browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

## Children's Privacy

Raani is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to provide valid consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent from a parent or guardian.

## International Transfers

Where personal information is transferred or stored outside Nigeria, we will take reasonable steps to ensure that it is protected in accordance with applicable data protection requirements.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after an update means the user accepts the revised policy.
